Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy stocks?

To buy stocks, go to the Trade page, select the stock symbol, choose "Buy" as the action, enter the quantity you want to purchase, and click "Execute Trade". The system will automatically calculate the total cost based on the current market price.

How do I sell stocks?

To sell stocks, go to the Trade page, select the stock symbol you own, choose "Sell" as the action, enter the quantity you want to sell, and click "Execute Trade". You can only sell stocks that you currently own in your portfolio.

How often are stock prices updated?

Stock prices are updated every 10 seconds to provide you with near real-time market data. You can see the last update time on the dashboard.

Can I view my trading history?

Yes! Go to the History page to view all your past trades, including buy and sell transactions, quantities, prices, and timestamps.

How do I track my portfolio performance?

Visit the Portfolio page to see all your current holdings, their current values, and your gain/loss for each position. The page also shows your total portfolio value and overall performance.

Is there a minimum or maximum trade amount?

Currently, there are no balance restrictions. You can buy or sell any quantity of stocks. The system is designed for demonstration purposes.

How do I contact support?

You can send us a message using the contact form below. Our admin team will review your message and respond as soon as possible.
